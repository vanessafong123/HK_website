Places to visit in

Hong Kong

For somewhere that's home to more than 7 million people and 260 islands, fast-paced is an understatement.

Hong Kong Disneyland is a great day out for everyone. The rides vary from kid-friendly ones to the extreme thrillers.

As gleaming towers rose from the historical architectures, Star Ferry has voyaged between Islands for 120 years.

Tai O is home to many relaxing cafes serving tea, coffee & snacks, some of which have tables overlooking the water.

Hike up to the Peak Galleria — along with classic picture-postcard skyline views across both sides of Victoria Harbour.

Take 268 steps to the Big Buddha, a 34 m-tall bronze statue and explore the nearby Po Lin Monastery.

No trip to Hong Kong is complete without trying dim sum - bite-sized snacks that are shared over tea.
